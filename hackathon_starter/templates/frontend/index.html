Welcome to FurryStay, this is an open source hostshareing service. It was an idea brought about with the mass amouts of social marketplaces that have been poping up. The idea behind this is everyone listed on here is volunteering their space and time in exchange for yours when they come visit you. Keeping in terms with the honor system. FurryStay is a listing service not a marketplace. This means that no money exchanges on FurryStay

What FurryStay is:
  • A place to meet people and make friends world wide.
  • A way to see new places.
  • A tool for meeting locals.
  • A way to score a free bed or a couch in exchange.
  • hella basic, opensources and driven by the community.

What FurryStay is not:
  • A free ride.
  • A paid service.
  • An airbnb service.
  • A gurantee.
also consider checking out pawpadsapp.com or getting the android app here to find locals near you.
