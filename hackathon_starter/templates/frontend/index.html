OpenStay

Welcome to openstay, this is an open source hostshareing service. It was an idea brought about with the mass abouts of social marketplaces to pop up. The idea behind this is everyone listed on here is volunteering their space and time in exchange for yours when they come visit you. Keeping in terms with the honor system. OpenStay is a listing service not a marketplace. This means that no money exchanges on OpenStay

What open stay is:
  • A place to meet people and make friends world wide.
  • A way to see new places.
  • A tool for meeting locals.
  • A way to score a free bed or a couch in exchange.
  • hella basic, opensources and driven by the community.

What open stay is not:
  • A free ride.
  • A paid service.
  • An airbnb service.
  • A gurantee.
